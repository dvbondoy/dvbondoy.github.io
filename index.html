Why Flick OS?

FlickOS is designed to be as light as possible so it can be used for older computers and save them from going to the landfill. as a result, your device will be more energy efficient, and system resources be utilized for more crucial tasks. But do not be discouraged by its lightness because FlickOS looks beautiful for old and new computers. Scroll down to learn more.
